HOW TO READ MEGA CAPS vs MARKET
MEGA P/C >> MARKET P/C Mega caps are seeing heavier put buying than the broad index. Institutions hedging concentrated positions in these names specifically — not a general market hedge.
MEGA P/C << MARKET P/C Mega caps seeing more call buying vs the index. Could mean rotation INTO mega caps while the broader market is hedged. Watch for index put buyers vs mega cap call buyers.
HIGH MEGA PREMIUM % vs LOW MARKET PREMIUM % Big money paying up for mega cap options while retail dominates broader index flow. Institutional conviction is concentrated in individual names, not the index.
HIGH MEGA PREMIUM PUTS Funds buying expensive puts on specific mega caps. Targeted downside protection or directional short bet — not general hedging.
HIGH MEGA PREMIUM CALLS Institutional call buying in mega caps. Often signals an anticipated catalyst. These players rarely pay premium without a reason.
MEGA LOTTO >> MARKET LOTTO Retail speculating on mega cap names more than the index. Common around earnings. Can cause gamma squeezes if dealers are short those strikes.
MEGA SENTIMENT DIVERGES FROM MARKET SENTIMENT Most actionable signal. When mega caps say BEARISH but the market says BULLISH, stocks driving 30%+ of the index are being quietly shorted. The index is likely to follow.

READING THE INDICATORS
^SKEW
Measures demand for OTM puts vs ATM options. Values above 130 signal heavy institutional hedging — smart money is buying tail-risk protection. SKEW > 140 = extreme fear of a black swan.
^VIX9D
9-day implied volatility. Spikes sharply around near-term events (FOMC, CPI, earnings). When VIX9D >> VIX, market expects a short-term shock but calm afterwards. Watch for mean reversion.
^VIX3M
3-month implied vol — the institutional planning horizon. When VIX3M > VIX, the term structure is in backwardation — funds expect sustained volatility, not a one-time shock. Historically a bearish signal.
^VIX6M
6-month implied vol. Compare vs VIX3M: if VIX6M > VIX3M, volatility is expected to increase — a longer-term bearish signal. Contango (VIX < VIX3M < VIX6M) = normal, calm market.
